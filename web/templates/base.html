What's this all about?

This website shows how 21 billion euros of farm subsidies paid in the United Kingdom between 2000 and 2007 were divided up among parliamentary constituencies.

The data was obtained by Jack Thurston, a co-founder of farmsubsidy.org, after requests to the UK Government, the Scottish Government, the Welsh Assembly Government and the Northern Ireland Executive under freedom of information legislation. A small amount of data was missing, incomplete, or contained errors and some information (such as full postcodes) was redacted by governments.

This is accounts for the 2.2 billion euros (around ten per cent of the total) where we have been unable to ascertain the recipient and/or a precise location. Data cleaning and structuring by Nils Mulvad. Website by Sym Roe and source code on GitHub Big thanks to theyworkforyou.com for the postcode-to-constituency data.

We hope the website is helpful. Any comments or questions: team@farmsubsidy.org
